About Dataset

This dataset has a collection of reports from Urban Park Rangers in New York. It has information about animals that are found in parks. The information includes the animal's condition, location, boroughs and more.

This dataset can help determine common locations these animals are found in

It can also show common conditions these animals are in whether its an injury or sickness.
